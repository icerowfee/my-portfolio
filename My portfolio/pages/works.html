Project Categories

Web Development December 2025

Sign Depot Company Website

This project involved designing and developing the official company website for The Sign Depot during my On-the-Job Training. The website was built using Laravel and Tailwind CSS, focusing on a clean layout, responsive design, and user-friendly navigation. The goal of the project was to present the company’s services clearly and professionally while improving its online presence. Through this project, I gained hands-on experience in full-stack web development, UI/UX design, and implementing real-world business requirements into a functional system.

Technologies Used:

HTML5 Laravel Tailwind CSS
Hardware Maintenance October 2025

Computer Cleaning

This task involved cleaning and maintaining one of the Account Executive’s office computers after it was reported to be running slowly. We disassembled the unit, performed thorough cleaning and cable management, and reassembled the computer properly. Having prior experience in computer assembly and disassembly helped us complete the task efficiently and restore the system’s performance.

Technologies/Tools Used:

Microsoft Word Cleaning Tools Screwdrivers
Computer Troubleshooting October 2025

Computer Troubleshooting

This task involved diagnosing and fixing a computer that was not displaying anything on the monitor due to a blue screen error. We performed troubleshooting procedures to identify the issue and successfully restored the system to proper working condition. This task strengthened our problem-solving skills and hands-on experience in computer maintenance.

Technologies/Tools Used:

Manual Testing
Other Tasks September 2025

Monthly Calendar Creation

This task involved creating a monthly calendar for the Account Executives (AEs) to help them track their scheduled events. I researched and utilized Canva templates to design a clear and organized layout, then printed the final output using the company’s shared printer. The calendar was designed to be practical, easy to read, and suitable for daily workplace use.

Technologies/Tools Used:

Canva
Other Tasks August 2025

Image Editing

This task was one of our initial assignments during the OJT. It involved reversing approximately 1,500 images and exporting them into TIF file format based on the client’s requirements. The project took nearly two days to complete and had to be redone twice due to changes in the given instructions. Through this task, we learned the importance of following updated guidelines, being flexible, and maintaining accuracy when handling large volumes of files.

Technologies/Tools Used:

CorelDraw
Other Tasks November 2025

Sticker Cutting

This project involved cutting two large rolls of label stickers for a client. Due to the volume of materials, the task took approximately one and a half weeks to complete. To improve efficiency and ensure accurate cutting, we coordinated with the HR department and used a paper cutter. This task required patience, teamwork, and attention to detail to meet the client’s requirements within the given timeframe.

Technologies/Tools Used:

Scissors Paper Cutter
